UGC Script · 30 Seconds
TikTok / Instagram Reels / Meta Paid
"The Dentist Didn't Tell Me This" — Dental Cosmetic
[0:00–0:03]HOOK: "I spent $3,000 at the dentist and got the same result from this $29 kit." (Shocked expression, hold product to camera)
[0:03–0:10]PROBLEM: "I was embarrassed to smile in photos. Coffee stains, years of buildup — dentist quoted $3,000. I said no way."
[0:10–0:20]SOLUTION: "Then I found [Brand]. Same professional-grade formula, same results from my bathroom. 7 days and I could not believe it." (Before/after reveal)
[0:20–0:27]PROOF: "Over 40,000 five-star reviews. My sister, mom and two coworkers have all ordered since I showed them mine."
[0:27–0:30]CTA: "Link in bio — limited offer right now. Don't wait." (Point to camera, full smile reveal)
VSL Script · 60 Seconds
Facebook / YouTube Pre-Roll / Landing Page
"Why Your Smile Is Costing You More Than Money" — Dental Cosmetic
[0:00–0:05]HOOK: "If you've ever covered your mouth when you laugh — this is for you." (Direct to camera, serious tone)
[0:05–0:20]AGITATE: "Most people don't realize how much a yellow smile costs them. Job interviews. First dates. Photos they delete before posting. The confidence you lose is the real price."
[0:20–0:40]SOLUTION + PROOF: "[Brand] uses the same hydrogen peroxide formula dentists charge thousands for. Clinical results in 7 days. 40,000+ verified reviews." (Testimonial clips)
[0:40–0:55]OFFER: "Right now — 30% off your first kit plus free shipping. Professional whitening for less than a dinner out."
[0:55–1:00]CTA: "Click below. Start smiling the way you were meant to." (Logo end card)
